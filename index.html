Visual Computing and Machine Learning Reading Group

Wednesdays from 16:15 until 17:05 in 1WN 3.11

This page contains information for the visual computing and machine learning reading group, which is a joint collaboration between the visual computing researchers in the CS department and the SAMBa group in the Maths department.

The idea is to present a paper you recently read that you think would be applicable/interesting for the group.

Everyone is welcome!

The Monday before you present, please send the organisers the paper title, the PDF (or a link that everyone can access), a paragraph summarizing the paper, and a paragraph explaining why the paper was chosen. Feel free to create some slides or other materials for your session. We will send a reminder email to the group on Tuesday with the papers and descriptions from the speakers. The idea is to allow 1-2 days for everyone to look at the paper prior to the meeting on Wednesday afternoon. Please remember to bring a copy of the paper (either electronic or a print out) to the session.
